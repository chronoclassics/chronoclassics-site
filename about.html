New York City

Operating out of Midtown Manhattan, I'm embedded in one of the world's most active watch markets. That means better access, better pricing, and the ability to source pieces most dealers can't find.

Authentication is everything

Every watch that passes through ChronoClassics is personally inspected by me. I know the tells — a refinished dial, a replaced crown, an incorrect serial range. If it doesn't check out, it doesn't go in my store. Full stop.

Physical Inspection

Case, crystal, crown, dial, and bracelet are examined closely for any signs of tampering, polishing, or non-original parts.

Serial & Movement Verification

The serial number is cross-referenced against known manufacturer records and production ranges. The movement is checked for originality.

Market Pricing

After authentication, I research recent comparable sales to ensure every price reflects true current market value — fair for both sides.
