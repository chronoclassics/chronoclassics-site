Our Story

Built from a lifelong passion for exceptional timepieces.

Some people collect stamps or coins. We collect watches.

From the moment we first held a Rolex Submariner — the weight of the case, the sweep of the seconds hand, the history sitting on your wrist — we were hooked. What started as a passion became an obsession, and eventually, a business we're genuinely proud of.

ChronoClassics was founded because we wanted to do something we love, and do it with integrity. We've spent years studying movements, tracking market values, and building relationships deep in New York's collector community. Every watch we acquire is evaluated personally. Every watch we sell, we stand behind completely.
