    Buyer Protection

    Return Policy

    We promise to do everything possible to make sure you are completely satisfied with your watch purchase. We also understand that sometimes it may not be a good fit, or simply it's not what you want. Unless otherwise indicated, you can return any watch for any reason within 14 days of receiving it.

    Original Condition Required

    All returned or exchanged items must be in their original condition as sold. All returned or exchanged items must include in the packaging: documents, instruments, links, straps, buckles, tags, protective stickers, boxes and warranties as shipped.

    No Modifications

    We ask that you do not alter any watch while it's in your possession. No modified watches will be accepted as a return. This includes intentional modifications such as exchanged, removed, or added parts, as well as unintentional modifications such as scratches, dents, or mechanical tampering.

    Unworn Condition

    Items must be returned without blemishes, dings, scratches or any indication that the watch has been worn or sized in any manner.
